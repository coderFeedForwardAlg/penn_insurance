Cooking Safety Tips
Download Content for Your Social Media
The graphics and captions below are available for you to use on your social media. The resources on this page can be used to encourage people to keep their family safe while cooking.
For the best quality graphic, we suggest you download images via Dropbox here.
By clicking on the image, or the "Download Image" link after the caption, you will be directed to a Dropbox page. From there, use the download button in the top right corner to save the image to your computer and add your logo to the image.
To download text, highlight the text, right click and select copy. Then, start a new post in your social media account, right click and select paste.
Add Agency Logo
The graphics available for download have the Penn National Insurance logo on them.
After you download a graphic, you may add your agency logo to it as shown in the example in the upper right.
Downloadable Content
Turkey fryers that use cooking oil are not safe. Use an infrared or electric fryer. Do not leave the fryer unattended. (Download Image)
Keep a lid nearby to smother small grease fires. Slid the lid over the pan and turn off the stovetop. (Download Image)
If you are simmering, baking or roasting food, check it regularly and remain in the kitchen while food is cooking. (Download Image)
Keep anything that can catch fire -- oven mitts, wooden utensils, food packaging, towels -- away from the stovetop. (Download Image)
When a cooking fire starts, get everyone out of the house and call for help outside. (Download Image)
