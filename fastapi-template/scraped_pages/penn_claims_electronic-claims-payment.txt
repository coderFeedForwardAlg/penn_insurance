Electronic Claims Payment
Payments within hours not days with our electronic payment options.
For personal auto and property claims, you have the option to have your claims payment sent electronically. Choose to receive your claim disbursement by:
ACH payment directly into your bank account
Push-to-Debit Card (sends your payment directlyt to your debit card)
Once the payment is entered into our system, you will receive an email with instruction to complete online security steps, followed by making your payment option. It's easy, secure and fast.
If you want to receive electronic payements, simply discuss with your claims representative.
